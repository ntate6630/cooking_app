How to - Cooking instructions

Baked salmon with herbs

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds salmon fillet, cut in half
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper, or to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced



Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Place salmon into baking dish, skin down. Top with pieces of butter.

Sprinkle with dried basil, salt and pepper

Top with lemon slices

Cover tightly with foil.

Bake 30-40 minutes, until flaky and pale pink inside (test with fork), serve hot
